# DPIA Checklist for Community Feature

Version: `2026-04-02`

## Why a DPIA is likely needed
- Closed health-related community
- Potential processing of special-category data
- Moderation and audit trails
- Risk of search-engine or disclosure harm if controls fail

## Assess
- Necessity and proportionality of each data field
- Visibility model for community data
- Risks from account compromise
- Risks from reporting and moderation tooling
- Withdrawal and deletion behaviour
- Staff access model

## Required outputs
- Risk register
- Mitigation catalogue
- Residual risk decision
- DPO/legal review record
