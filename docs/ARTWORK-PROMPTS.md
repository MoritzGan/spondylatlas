## 🏷️ Logo

### Logo-Variante 1 — Atlas-Symbol
```
Minimalist logo for "SpondylAtlas", a medical community platform for ankylosing spondylitis.
Symbol: a stylized human spine seen from behind, gently curving like the letter S,
composed of smooth rounded vertebrae shapes stacked vertically.
The spine forms an abstract atlas/map pin shape at the top.
Warm amber color (#D97706) on clean white background.
Geometric, modern, approachable — NOT clinical or scary.
Single-color vector style, suitable for app icon.
No text.
```

### Logo-Variante 2 — Gemeinschafts-Symbol
```
Minimalist logo icon for a patient community platform called "SpondylAtlas".
Abstract symbol: two human silhouettes (side profile, simplified) sitting together,
their spines forming a gentle arch that resembles a bridge or a sunrise.
Warm amber gradient from #D97706 to #F59E0B.
Clean white background, rounded shapes, friendly and human.
No text, suitable as app icon 512x512.
```

### Logo-Variante 3 — Atlas + Wirbelsäule
```
Clean vector logo: the letter "S" stylized as a human spine with 5-6 rounded disc shapes,
warm amber color, slightly glowing.
Below it, the word "SpondylAtlas" in a modern serif font (similar to Lora).
Background: off-white / warm parchment (#F5F0E8).
Medical but warm — like a friendly doctor's practice, not a pharma brand.
```
